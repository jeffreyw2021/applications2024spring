Soccer App Design Notes

App Feedback and Features

HEJA - Coaching Tool

Primary Use: Communication with teams.

Issues Identified:

  • Messages are not always read.
  • Parents do not always pay attention or respond to RSVPs.
  • The app does not effectively support multiple teams, age groups, or multiple players per parent.

Desired Features:

  • Enhanced notification options for better engagement.
  • Support for multiple teams and players per parent.
  • Promotion of a culture of engagement among users.

TeamSnap

Features:

  • Schedule creation with customizable rules.
  • Player profiles that include parental information.
  • Volunteer sign-up and RSVP management.
  • No advertisements, unlike Sports Engine.

Injury Management

Emergency contact numbers are readily available, allowing for quick response times in the event of an injury.

Feedback from Players:

  • Availability of first-aid equipment and personnel is limited.

League Administration Tools and Responsibilities

Role of a League Administrator

  • Provides commissioner support.
  • Utilizes external tools for marketing, like Mailchimp, to reduce the need for dedicated personnel.
  • Automates financial tasks to streamline operations.

Suggested App Features

A "When-Is-Good" feature to assist with scheduling by:

  • Calculating transportation time.
  • Integrating auto-linked communication for better coordination.
